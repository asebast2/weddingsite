OUR STORY

Our story began at a hotel bar somewhere in downtown Baltimore. Anish was at a work event when Pablo (his best man) decided to make Anish’s love life, or lack there of, the topic of conversation. That’s when Tamika Auguste decided to step in and heroically save the day. It was almost as though she was born for this, and very quickly she start asking a series of questions. Before you knew it she had someone in mind!

That same night Lauren was on call at Georgetown University Hospital when the text messages about this single entrepreneur started pouring in. She had heard about Tamika's matchmaking skills and agreed to give it a try.

A few months passed by and what started off as a promising match began to dwindle as the two could not get their schedules to line up. Anish decided to give it one last try and invited Lauren to his office Christmas party. He wore his best suit but a few hours into the party there was no sign of Lauren! Meanwhile, back at the hospital, Lauren was frantically trying to get out on time, and had to make one last stop in the OR to hand off the pager to her junior resident. The attending spotted her and asked her to assist on the case as they could always use an extra set of hands. As any good resident would, she went to scrub in, knowing she'd definitely miss the party. Back in the OR her junior resident knew she had to do something, and told the attending all about the big date that night. Much to her surpise, the grumpy old attending had a soft spot in his heart, and when Lauren came back in he told her to get home so she could get ready for her date! A Christmas Miracle!

After all the drama they finally met. Anish locked eyes with her for the first time, his heart skipped a beat. He knew. He knew that she was the one.

The rest, as they say, is history!
