THE CREW

THE BRIDESMAIDS

Kirstin

Kristin and Lauren met in organic chemistry class at Fordham University. They were the perfect study partners, living two doors down in Finley Hall, though they probably spent more hours in the library. Over the years they shared bunk beds and many memories in the “tri-bar area.” They both migrated south to DC and Lauren is so fortunate to have such a great friend right around the corner.

Alexandra

Alexandra and Lauren met in elementary school. You could always find Lauren riding her bike over to Alexandra’s house to build igloos in the winter or play Nintendo and pool in the summer. Lauren was part of the family, Alexandra’s dad even consulted her while making the grocery list. Through all of life’s ups and downs they’ve been by each other’s side.

Sonia

Sonia and Lauren met in medical school. They made it through the island making s’mores on the beach, watching shooting stars on the dock, and avoiding catastrophe while making Dominica Family Thanksgiving Dinner. They could laugh for hours together, even if no one else around them thinks they’re funny. Lauren doesn’t know how see could have made it off the island without Sonia.

Farhana

Farhana and Lauren met on their psychiatry rotation in medical school. Their friendship blossomed while leading the in-patient group dance class to the beats of Rihanna. Farhana and Lauren have created great memories eating their way through Charleston and zip lining through Costa Rica. She has always been there to remind Lauren to not take life too seriously and roll with the punches.

Rachel

Lauren met Rachel when she moved to DC for residency. They have shared many memories from Christmas parties at the Clark’s house to wine tours around Northern Virginia. Rachel is always ready to lend a helping hand, even scoping out her neighborhood for a new apartment for Lauren. Lauren is excited to start this new chapter with Rachel by her side.

Renee

Renee and Lauren met during their OB/GYN residency…even though they both hated OB . They bonded during wellness walks through the Georgetown campus and climbing to the top of the forbidden cupola. Renee has always been there to support Lauren, from giving her IV fluids in call room when she had the flu to telling her to keep Anish around when they first started dating. They have been there for each others career lows and celebrated their great successes, and will always be there of one another.

THE GROOMSMAN

Pablo

Pablo met Anish as a Junior Associate at Deloitte, when Anish caught him red handed working “a non work related project” --That project would turn out to be the beginning of a partnership that world propel them to new heights, including their journey down the entrepreneurial path. Anish is very thankful to have Pablo as a trusted confidant and looks foward to future adventures together!

Anup

Both Anup and Anish, strongly believe that #twinning = #winning. Growing up together they tried endlessly to use ESP to communicate with each other, and when that failed, they resorted the secret twin-bond. Weather it was playing cricket on the streets of Mumbai or working through major life decisions they both have had and will always have each other's back!

Arun

As big brother, Arun has a very special place in Anish’s life. Arun had the distinct pleasure of introducing Anish to his first taste of gambling and ‘real life’. Anish and Arun both have grown closer in the recent years, and Anish looks foward to munching on a lot more of Arun's World Famous Egg Roll's

Jake

Somewhere between discussing Nietzsche’s Übermensch and figuring out the best opening line on tinder, it was clear that Anish and Jake were going to hit it off. Both share a passion for life and curiosity for not just how things are but how they can be better. Anish is very grateful for everything that Jake has done for him, and looks forward dancing in the land of the giants!

Nihar

As a young struggling resident, Nihar made the mistake of taking up Anish on his offer for free dinner. During dinner Nihar and Anish formed a bond, which has grown to a deep and strong friendship. Their friendship spans everything from geeking out over the coolest tech, the best ways to meditate, and even to preeclampsia guidance! Anish continues to have dinner with Nihar on a routine basis —> although now they split the bill since Nihar is a 'real' doctor :)

Greg

Anish met Greg in Amsterdam. Well, it was more like Amsterdam Falafel in Adam’s Morgan. Greg had just returned from a mission trip in Nicaragua and Anish had recently left his job at Deloitte. Both were turning a new chapter in their lives. This allowed them to confide in each other and share their experiences about life, work, and balance. Anish is especially thankful of Greg’s spiritual guidance in his life.
